A Legacy of
Transformation

After almost 17 years as CEO of the V&A Waterfront, David Green stepped down from the role, leaving behind a legacy of growth, placemaking, and public value. Under his leadership, the Waterfront strengthened its position as a world-class destination for tourism, culture, innovation, and investment.

As a City Ambassador for Cape Town, David continues to champion the city internationally, helping position it as a place of opportunity, creativity, and global relevance.
