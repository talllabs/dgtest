Profile

Leadership with a sense of place

David Green is known for destination leadership, strategic development, and championing Cape Town as a world-class hub for tourism, culture, and investment.

After nearly 17 years as CEO of the V&A Waterfront, David Green stepped into an advisory role in January 2026, closing a defining chapter of stewardship at one of Africa’s most iconic mixed-use destinations.
